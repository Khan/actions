Bug-finding recall is the workflow's load-bearing metric, and the 2026-07-20 pooled A/B's recall gain (83% to 90%, true misses down 43%) concentrated in correctness-adjacent rows, so the 2x per-token price goes to the one role where the measurement supports it.
